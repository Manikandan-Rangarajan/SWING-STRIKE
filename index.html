SWING STRIKE

Description & Rules

Step up to the plate, adjust your stance, and swing for the fences in Swing Strike! Strategize your moves, aim for successive runs, and accumulate points to become the ultimate baseball champion.   • Runs and Points: In Swing Strike, there are a total of six runs, numbered from 1 to 6. Your goal is to score runs and accumulate points while preventing the computer from doing the same. Each successful run adds points to your overall score.
  • Strikes: Whenever both you and the computer roll successive numbers, it's called a "strike." The key is to avoid accumulating three successive strikes in a row, as that will result in an "out" for your batting side. It's all about strategic decision-making to keep the inning alive and maintain your momentum.
  • Number Matches: Swing Strike introduces an exciting element of number matches. If both you and the computer roll the same number during an at-bat, your batting score becomes a combination of your score and the computer's score. This adds a thrilling dimension of strategy as you aim to maximize your points and outsmart the computer.
